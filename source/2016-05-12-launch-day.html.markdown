Launch days are usually underwhelming.  After several launch days I've learned that people tend to overestimate external interest.  This is especially true for apps that at this point take a considerable amount of effort to persuade potential users to go through the trouble of searching for your app name and hitting "Get."

Just having an app in the AppStore no longer means anything. I worked on an app  for a startup that was present in the store in beta mode for over a year and *no one noticed*.  The app officially "launched" a year later with tons of marketing with a very tepid response.  The larger point: you could develop a perfect app but its nothing with proper messaging and distribution.
