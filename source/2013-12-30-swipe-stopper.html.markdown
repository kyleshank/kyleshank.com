title:  "Swipe Stopper"
date:   2013-12-30 22:56:00
---

Swipe Stopper is an iOS app I made based on a joke.  Instead of it ending there I decided to follow through and actually make the app.

The joke is based on a common situation when sharing photos with a friend.  It goes something like this:

1. You hand over your phone to a friend to share a photo or two.
2. The friend swipes beyond the photo(s) you intended to share.
3. You don't know what to do.
4. The friend ends up seeing some photos you didn't intend for them to see.
5. The friend comments on these photos.
6. Shame.
7. You motion to take the phone back but the friend ignores.
8. The friend ends up swiping through all of the photos on your phone.

Swipe Stopper is a solution to this problem.  The app allows you to select the photos you want to share *before* handing over your phone.  The friend will only be able to see the photos you've shared and nothing more.  They won't be able to exit the photo display mode and return to the app.  They are stuck looking at just the photos you've selected.  There is a secret gesture to return to the app once the friend has handed the phone back.

It goes without saying that whoever would do this would be a *terrible* friend.  People are nosy and not just friends.  Imagine sharing an iPad with photos at a family gathering.  Wouldn't you feel better knowing that the iPad is only displaying the photos you've selected as its passed from aunt to uncle to grandma and nephew?
