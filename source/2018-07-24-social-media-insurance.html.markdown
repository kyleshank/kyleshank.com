---
title: Social Media Insurance
date: 2018-07-24 07:41 EDT
tags:
---

I think social media introduces an extraordinary amount of liability for both individuals and businesses.  One dumb tweet can get a person fired or crash the stock of a corporation.  I predict there will be demand for social media insurance products in the future.  

Individuals may want to cover themselves against the risk of termination, discrimination or profiling based on their social media usage.  Tweets from 10 years ago that you forgot about could get you fired tomorrow.

Businesses will need to insure against the risk of harm due to posts made on the company accounts and the posts made by employees on their personal accounts.  Losing valuable employees as a result of social media usage could also pose real risk.

Every post made on social media has the potential to produce immediate or future liability.
