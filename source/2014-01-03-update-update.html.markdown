Version 1.1.1 of [Cloudgram](https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/cloudgram/id739501813?ls=1&mt=8) was just released on the AppStore.

The big changes in 1.1+ are cosmetic.

The "Home" tab is now called "Timeline."

I went from a custom black TabBar with icons only back to the native iOS7 TabBar with both icons and titles.  The reason I had a custom TabBar in the first place was to have a camera button in the middle for easy access to the upload dialog.  Now the camera (upload) button is on the upper right of the Timeline tab.  An option to upload is also within the action menu when viewing an album.

A few other bugs were fixed along with some performance improvements to the photo & video selection screen when uploading.
