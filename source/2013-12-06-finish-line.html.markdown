I am at the finish line with Cloudgram.

I have delayed its release enough.  I decided to go ahead and take the past month to build the Android app.  It's done.  Now there are no more excuses.

Real artists ship.  Cloudgram will ship next week.  Then I'll immediately move on to the next idea.
