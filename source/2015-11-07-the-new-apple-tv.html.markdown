---
title:  "The New Apple TV"
date:   2015-11-07 18:36:00
---
I was one of the lucky ones who got access to the new Apple TV hardware through the Apple Developer program back in September.  I played around with the tvOS SDK and took a shallow dive into some of the sample apps.  I used the Apple TV hardware here and there since; streaming content from my iPhone or Mac and playing with some of the built-in apps to get a feel for the UX.

Today I finally got around to installing the tvOS update with a functioning AppStore and the everything clicked.  The UX, the remote, and the apps all finally produced the experience I was looking for.  My plan to spend a few minutes exploring turned into hours.  TV Apps are strangely engaging.

I was surprised to see the Airbnb and TripAdivsor apps at launch but planning a trip from the couch makes sense.  Fitness and instruction apps are also perfect for the TV platform.
