The bottleneck is never what you think it is.

Development capacity is often blamed as the reason something isn't done yet.  More often than not though the reason something isn't done yet is because you can't decide what to build.  Instead of focusing on core product values many opt out of the hard work of figuring out the product and decide to simply build every idea and feature.

Deciding what to build is always harder than actually building it.
