---
title: Turning off social media
date: 2016-07-17 11:32 EDT
tags:
---
I'm blocking myself from accessing Facebook and Twitter.

I've installed Simple Blocker for Chrome and removed the apps from my phone.

Day to day news and the reaction to it has gotten to me.  I probably check my feeds every 30 minutes or so for no real reason.  The interaction rarely brings me any real joy and more often than not affects my mood negatively.

I'm not opting out of news; I'll just continue to gather from sources outside of social media.  Maybe I won't be the very first person to know something but I don't have to be either.  The news can wait.

I may publish on Twitter or Facebook from time to time to promote something I'm doing because they are primary distribution channels.  Otherwise, I'm turning social media off.
