The Puppy
=========

![Link in a clever sweater](/images/link.png)

Just a few weeks after getting married Amy and I got a long-haired mini-dachshund puppy.  We named him Link after the main character in the Zelda series of video-games.

Link is my first dog.  I have only temporarily had hermit crabs and a turtle before this.  There was definitely a learning curve to raising a puppy but it has been a great learning experience.  It is a bit like having a baby which will hopefully serve me well in the future.

Link is a sweet boy who just wants to play and sleep all the time.

The Apps
========

Over the past few years I have been contracted to build several iPhone apps.  This summer I got back on that horse and learned how to ride another in Android.

I developed an [iOS app](https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/swipe-stopper/id680261908?ls=1&mt=8) based on a joke called [Swipe Stopper](https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/swipe-stopper/id680261908?ls=1&mt=8).  The joke is based around the bad phone etiquette many people seem to have of swiping through all of your photos when you only meant to show them a few. Swipe Stopper allows you to select the photos you want to show to your friends, family or co-workers before handing over your phone.  They will only be able to view the photos you selected and nothing more.  Swipe Stopper is an App For That.

I also developed an [iOS app](https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/majority-report/id690931703?ls=1&mt=8) and [Android app](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.samsedershow.majorityreport) for the [Majority Report](http://majority.fm).  The Majority Report is a live radio show and podcast hosted by Sam Seder.  The goal of the app was to provide a live streaming and podcast experience that made tuning in to the Majority Report as simple as possible.  In addition to listening to the show users can watch live video and even instant message the host.  Additionally, Sam can send push notifications to all iOS and Android users to let them know the topic of the day and which guests will be on.
