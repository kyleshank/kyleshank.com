---
title: Personalization is a Problem
date: 2016-05-06 10:07 EDT
tags:
---
We all live in a filter bubble.  Our streams of information are curated to our likes.  We tend to follow people we agree with and block those we don't.  We all maintain an illusion that we control what we consume but in reality we are greatly influenced by algorithms running social networks and search results.

Personalization is a problem because it can isolate us in a world of comfortable information.  As humans we seek gratification and personalization seeks to give us more of it.  It creates a world of convenient facts and lets us avoid ideas or narratives that don't conform to our world view.

The ultimate problem: we are lazy as shit.  Personalization helps our lazy asses consume more information than we would otherwise but at some cost.

My solution: anti-personalization.  A service that analyzes my streams of information and creates a feed that has *nothing* to do with anything I *like*.  I bet it will be interesting and I'll be a more well-rounded person as a result.

Make it happen!
