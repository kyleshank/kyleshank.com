Today I released [Cloudgram](https://cloudgram.com).

[What is Cloudgram?](/2013/12/09/what-is-cloudgram.html)  Cloudgram is an easy way to privately share photos and videos with your friends and family.

It took me 3 months of sweat to build both mobile apps and the Rails based web app and API.

Check out the front page of [cloudgram.com](https://cloudgram.com) for a full description of the product.
