title:  "What is Cloudgram?"
date:   2013-12-09 23:27:00
---

Cloudgram is an easy way to privately share photos and videos with your friends and family.

Cloudgram will be available as an iOS and Android app.  It was designed for both the iPhone and iPad as well as Android phones and tablets.

Cloudgram.com is a full featured web version of the service that also can work on mobile devices that aren't iOS or Android based.

Cloudgram allows users to create private albums and control who can view them.  Users invite friends by quickly selecting contacts on their phone or by entering in individual email addresses.  Friends will receive the invitation by email containing a private link to a web version of the album that they can view immediately without having to install the Cloudgram app.

If a friend has the Cloudgram app and accepts the invitation they will get the added benefit of getting push notifications whenever the album is updated.

Multiple people can collaborate on an album if friends are made contributors.

Cloudgram launches on December 12th, 2013.
