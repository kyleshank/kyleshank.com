---
title:  "Podcast of the week: improv4humans"
date:   2015-11-09 02:20:00
---
[The best podcast I listened to this week was improv4humans](http://www.earwolf.com/episode/dan-deacon/).  The episode is  an insanely impressive example of long-form/harold-esque improv.  I was amazed by the high level of group-think going on, the depths they went to continually mine a single thread where there was seemingly nothing and found everything.  The end result was complex, layered, thematic and silly all at the same time.

Improv is hit or miss.  This was a hit.
